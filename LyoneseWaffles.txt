Ingredients
-----------
250 grams  of white flour
25 grams   of white sugar
1 pinch    of salt
75 grams   of butter
37.5 cl    of milk
3             eggs

Optionnal :
1 spoon    of Rhum


-Melt the butter and let it cool
-Mix salt, sugar, flour, and butter
-Add egg yolks and rhum
-Make sure it's well mixed
-Beat the egg whites (hey need to be stiff)
-Incorporate whites into the mix

Bake. Make sure waffle are not completely cooked inside.
